Since Conception

History of Anniyappa Publications

Anniyappa Publications began as an academic publishing house with a fundamental mission: to provide highly precise, structurally logical, and affordable reference textbooks for students pursuing technical streams. Recognising that computing and engineering curricula evolve rapidly, we focused on producing works that synthesize theoretical standard frameworks with immediate industrial implementations.

Over the years, we have grown to host a vast portfolio of titles spanning Core Software Engineering, Modern Artificial Intelligence, Web Development Architecture, IoT Sensors, and Cloud Computing Services. By collaborating directly with professors and industry practitioners, we maintain strict editorial accuracy and real-world compliance in every book we release.

"A good textbook bridges the classroom and the workspace. Our goal is to ensure that bridge is solid, clear, and modern."

Industry-Academia Nexus

Introducing SB Institute

SB Institute was established under the leadership and vision of Dr. C.V. Suresh Babu. A pioneer in technical pedagogy, Dr. Suresh Babu conceptualized SB Institute as an energetic academic incubator designed to bridge the structural gaps between academic syllabi and actual corporate engineering cycles.

Through collaborative projects, guest lectures, FDP workshops, and certification streams, SB Institute works with universities across South India. The institution guarantees that every student intern graduates not merely with credit papers, but with a fully built product portfolio demonstrating advanced hands-on capability.
