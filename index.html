Who We Are

Anniyappa Publications & SB Institute

Bridging the Gap Between Traditional Academic Syllabi and Current Industry Demands.

Founded with the philosophy of providing high-quality, practical educational aids, Anniyappa Publications publishes specialized curriculum guides and engineering textbooks. Collaborating with the SB Institute, led by industry expert Dr. C.V. Suresh Babu, we design intensive workshops, seminars, and certification programs that provide learners with concrete, project-focused abilities.

Learn More About Us

Our Mission

To author and distribute technically precise, highly readable academic resources and deliver structured, project-centric internships that equip students with future-ready corporate skills.

Our Vision

To become a premier knowledge hub in technology education, renowned for transforming standard classroom studies into real-world project portfolios that pave direct paths to corporate employment.
